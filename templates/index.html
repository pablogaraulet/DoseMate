About Us

At DoseMate, we believe that effective medication management is essential to ensure the well-being and safety of patients, especially in settings where every dose counts. Our innovative system leverages the power of IoT to provide a seamless medication reminder solution that not only alerts patients at the right time but also logs every dose administration and communicates real-time updates to caregivers.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between modern technology and compassionate healthcare. With a robust backend powered by Flask and an easy-to-navigate frontend, DoseMate is designed to support both patients and healthcare providers, ensuring that medication schedules are followed precisely. Whether you're a caregiver or a patient, DoseMate offers a reliable, accessible solution that empowers you to manage your health with confidence. Join us in transforming medication management for a healthier future.
